Daniel Waller.

Daniel has been the manager of My Fairy Godmother's Cakes for 12 years and has helped the business develop and branch off to become a bigger and better business. Since Daniel's takeover in 2002 he has hired more staff to help customers get the cakes for their special days.

Amy Millard.

Amy has been working for My Fairy Godmothers Cakes for 10 years and works in the kitten which is where the amazing creations are made. In the time she has been working for Fairy Godmothers Cakes she has developed her skills as a cake maker and has also began becoming a customer consultant and visiting the customers to get an idea of what the customer would like us to make.
